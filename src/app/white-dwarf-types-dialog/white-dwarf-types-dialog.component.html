Notes

  • Atmospheric type does not indicate core composition; most white dwarfs have carbon–oxygen cores.
  • Spectral types can change over time as the star cools and atmospheric layers mix.
  • DZ white dwarfs are of particular interest because their metal lines often indicate accretion from planetary debris.
