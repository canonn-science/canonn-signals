What is {{ data.type === 'apo' ? 'apoapsis' : 'periapsis' }}?

Apoapsis is the point in the orbit farthest from the parent body; periapsis is the closest point. Their distances are computed from the orbital semi-major axis a and eccentricity e using:

Periapsis: a × (1 - e)
Apoapsis: a × (1 + e)
