What does this mean?

These aren't real physics events. The stellar forge is programmed to prevent collisions, but a few slip through. When two objects are on a collision course, they simply pass straight through each other and carry on in their orbits. It's not supported by the engine — what happens is the two bodies intersect and create a non-game experience of meshes. Any permanent change (a real explosion or new asteroid field) only happens when Frontier manually writes it into a server update.

Overlap is the depth of the intersection at closest approach, as a percentage of the bodies' combined radii: 0% means the surfaces merely graze, 100% means the centres pass through one another.
