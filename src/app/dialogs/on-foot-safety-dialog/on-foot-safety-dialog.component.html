On-foot safety thresholds:

  • < 182 K — battery drain risk. The suit's thermal management cannot cope with extreme cold and battery life is severely reduced.
  • 182 – 699 K — safe to disembark.
  • ≥ 700 K — risk of injury or death. The suit cannot protect against extreme heat.
  • > 2.7 g — disembarking is blocked by the SRV bay regardless of temperature.

How are the estimated temperatures calculated?
The min and max figures come from a dataset of 3,301,198 organic temperature measurements collected from EDSM and in-game journal data. For each reading the on-foot temperature was compared against the body's surface temperature to produce a deltaT value. The 5th and 95th percentiles of these deltas give a ~90% coverage interval:

Est. min = surface temperature + p5 deltaT
Est. max = surface temperature + p95 deltaT
